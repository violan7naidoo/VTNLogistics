Vehicles Transportation

VTN LOGISTICS has been performing vehicle shipping for decades and has developed a reputation as a safe, quality vehicle transportation company that provides excellent customer service to individuals and corporations. Our professional staff members are trained in the safe, timely transport of various types of vehicle including motorcycles, boats, etc. Enclosed and open vehicle transportation requires specialized equipment in order to safely protect and transport your vehicle. Our fleet of modern equipment has been specifically designed for maximum capacity to provide an attractive combination of outstanding quality and competitive pricing. We guarantee to complete your vehicle shipping requirements on time and damage-free. We also offer expertise in customs document completion and clearance for vehicle shipping. Upon the entry of vehicles into one of our warehouses a full and complete dock receipt is issued. Pictures are taken and copies are e-mailed to our customers. We make sure your vehicle is taken care. Securing vehicles during transit is one of the most important stages of any international shipment. Our warehouse team are professionally trained and our equipment is specialized. You can be confident that your vehicle will arrive at its destination in the same condition it was in when you handed it over to us.

Air Freight

We provide both domestic and international air freight cargo delivery, with a clear statement outlining the transit duration and tracking service for your convenience. Whether there is a need for a consolidated or direct air service, our experts are capable of transporting your goods to or from almost any point. Whether you require a door-to-door or airport-to-airport service, we will tailor our service to meet your deadlines. Our air freight cargo delivery division is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We are always there to provide you with the best possible freighting opportunities and services.

Road Freight

We provide both domestic and international door-to-door ground freight transportation, be it a full truck load or less than a truck load, with a clear statement outlining the transit duration and tracking service for your convenience. Whether there is a need for a consolidated or direct road service, our experts are capable of transporting your goods. A variety of options are available for the most competitive and reliable ground freight forwarding service. For freight consignments that require a full truck load, we dedicate a van or truck for the entire move, which is from the shipper's door or warehouse to when the freight is received by the recipient at the final destination. The type of equipment used depends on the type of freight being shipped, its weight and dimensions. For shipments requiring less than a trailer load, the freight is consolidated in our contracted vans and transported efficiently to the recipient's door. Transit times and service availability varies according to the city, the description of the freight, and its weight and volume. Most shipments are handled door-to-door for business-to-business, but arrangements can be made for residential pick-up or delivery for an additional fee. Our road freight cargo delivery division is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We are always there to provide you with the best possible ground cargo shipping services.
